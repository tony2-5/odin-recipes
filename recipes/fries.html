Crispy French Fries

    Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Oil

    Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into desired french fry shape.
  2. Parboil potatoes in salted water.
  3. Pat potatoes dry.
  4. Drop potatoes in frying oil and remove once golden brown.
  5. Place fries on plate with paper towel to absorb excess oil and season with salt while hot

    6. Enjoy!
