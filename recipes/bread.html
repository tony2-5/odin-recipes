Rustic Bread

    Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Sugar

    Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in mixing bowl.
  2. Stir until combined and dough ball forms.
  3. Put dough on floured surface and knead for 6 minutes.
  4. Place dough in bowl and cover, allow to rest in warm area for 2 hours or until doubled in size
  5. Deflate the dough and pat into rectangle shape
  6. Fold the edges of the dough into the center and roll into log shape.
  7. Place dough on baking tray and allow to rest for 45 minutes after risen add diagnal slashes across the loave.
  8. Bake the bread and allow the bread to cool down before cutting

    9. Enjoy!
