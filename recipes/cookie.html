COOKIES!

    Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Egg
  • Vanilla
  • Chocolate Chips

    Steps

  1. In one bowl combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl combine soften butter, sugars and mix until fluffy.
  3. Beat in the egg and vanilla to the bowl with the butter and sugars.
  4. Stir in the flour mixture and finally stir in the chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop cookie dough onto baking sheet with a few inches of seperation between each.
  6. Bake and then cool completely.

    7. Enjoy!
