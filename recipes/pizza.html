Delicious Pizza!

Ingredients

Dough

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Yeast
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Sugar

Toppings

  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Combine warm water, yeast, and sugar into a mixing bowl to allow yeast to bloom.
  2. Once yeast visibly is bloomed add flour, salt, and oil to mixing bowl.
  3. Stir mixture until a ball of dough is formed.
  4. Massage dough to form for 5 minutes and allow to rest in warm place.
  5. Once dough doubled in size stretch and toss into desired pizza shape
  6. Top with tomotato sauce, cheese, and basil.
  7. Cook in pizza oven high heat, making sure to rotate and evenly cook.

    8. Enjoy!
